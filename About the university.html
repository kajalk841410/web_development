Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, formerly known as West Bengal University of Technology, is a public state university located in Kalyani, West Bengal, India. It is funded completely by the Government of West Bengal. Wikipedia Founded: August 7, 2001 Slogan: "(In Pursuit of Knowledge and Excellence)" Location: Haringhata 741249 Total Enrollment: 2,964 .
